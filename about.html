About Me

Hiya, folks! My name is Jamie Bourgeois; owner, photographer and editor behind Jbphotos. I wanna start by saying thanks for stopping in to check me out! You don't know how much it means. Growing up, I was raised to put others first, to strive to help people and to create meaningful relationships. I want to take those values and build them into my photography. I focus on creating a personal atmosphere with my clients. I aim to capture the most intimate moments in life while not breaking the bank. Most importantly, I strive to help you make your memories last forever. In closing, I love the connections I make through my photography and I can't wait to help capture the sweetest moments that have yet to come. See you on the other side of my camera!

XOXO- Jamie
